Latest Travis Scott News and Updates

Travis Scott's latest song joins the hottest new club in popular music

Travis Scott is once again dominating the music scene as his latest track, "4X4," has debuted at No. 1 on the Billboard Hot 100, marking a major milestone in his career. The track became the 83rd song in history to debut at the top of the chart, showcasing Scott’s continued influence in the music industry. His signature blend of energetic production and catchy hooks has earned him a dedicated following, and "4X4" is no exception. With this new achievement, Travis Scott further solidifies his position as one of the biggest stars in hip-hop today.

Playboi Carti And Travis Scott Deliver An Instant Classic With "Crush"

Travis Scott makes a major impact on Playboi Carti’s new album, MUSIC, which dropped on Friday. The 30-track project features a range of sounds, but Scott’s presence is one of the biggest highlights. He appears early on the album with CRUSH, a song that was previously teased on Instagram. The track builds with hypnotic synths before Scott helps drive it to an explosive beat drop, setting the stage for Carti’s deep-toned verse. As Carti pushes his sound in a new direction, Scott’s signature energy elevates the track, proving once again why he remains one of hip-hop’s most influential artists.
