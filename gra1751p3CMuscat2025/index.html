Miami Theater Central has been at the forefront of the city's vibrant arts scene, showcasing a diverse array of performances and nurturing local talent. With a commitment to both classic and contemporary works, the theater has become a cultural landmark, providing an inclusive and dynamic space for artists and audiences alike. Its rich history and ongoing dedication to excellence make it a cherished institution in the heart of Miami.

Alice’s Adventures in Wonderland” at the Miami Theater Center in Miami Shores is a home-grown twist on Lewis Carroll’s mind-bending classic. With a colorful cast, a multimedia set, and original music, this take on “Alice” puts the M-I-A in wonderland.This adaptation offers a fresh perspective on Alice's journey through the whimsical world of Wonderland. The talented cast brings the story to life, while the vibrant set and original music create an engaging experience for audiences. Join Alice as she encounters quirky characters and explores the magic of imagination in this captivating production!
